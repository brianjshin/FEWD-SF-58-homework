Cool & Spicy Kimchi Dip

All Recipes

Health Benefits of Kimchi

Where to Buy

Ingredients

  • 15 oz. (1 bottle) King's Kimchi
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 0.7 oz. (1/2 packet) Dry vegetable soup mix
  • 3/4 cup mayonnaise
  • Black pepper
  • Crackers, sourdough bread, or vegetables

Preparation

  1. Chop Kimchi into 1/8" - 1/4" pieces and drain.
  2. Mix first four ingredients in large mixing bowl.
  3. Add pepper to taste.
  4. Fold sides. Roll up tightly, placing seam side down on plate.
  5. Serve on crackers, with sourdough bread, or with vegetables.
