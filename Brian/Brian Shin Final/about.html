Our Story

In 1966 our parents Inja Kim and Jung Taek Wang, both Korean immigrants, wanted to share their beloved tradition of kimchi with the city of San Francisco. They worked tirelessly, even selling their delicious kimchi from the back of their car. They truly believed kimchi brought something special to any meal.

Where to Buy

Soon, loyal customers were asking for more and more of their original-recipe, healthy kimchi. Our parents could barely prepare the kimchi fast enough. So, in 1970, our parents answered the demand by starting what would become King's Asian Gourmet Kimchi. They even took the extra care of importing cabbage seeds from Korea for a crisper, sweeter taste.

A Family Tradition Continues

Fast forward 44 years and King's Kimchi is still family-owned.
