Parmesan Kimchi Firecracker Dip

All Recipes

Health Benefits of Kimchi

Where to Buy

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup (1-16 oz. jar) King's Kimchi
  • 1 cup shredded parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • Crackers or sourdough bread

Optional Toppings

  • Fresh basil or watercress leaves
  • Black sesame seeds

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to broil.
  2. Chop Kimchi into 1/4" pieces and drain.
  3. Mix all ingredients in large mixing bowl.
  4. Place mixture into small oven safe bowls or ramekins.
  5. Place into preheated oven for 10-15 minutes until top becomes crispy and golden brown.
  6. Cool on wire rack 5 minutes.
  7. Serve on crackers or with sourdough bread.
  8. Sprinkle additional parmesan cheese on top.

Optional

  • Place a small leaf of basil or watercress over cheese.
  • Sprinkle black sesame seeds.
