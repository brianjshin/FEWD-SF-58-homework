Kimchi Spring Rolls

All Recipes

Health Benefits of Kimchi

Where to Buy

Ingredients

  • 14 oz. King's Kimchi
  • 4 oz. Dry rice stick noodles
  • Romaine lettuce cut into 4" pieces
  • 1 lb. Thinly sliced beef (marinated/cooked)
  • Dried seaweed cut into 1/4" wide strips.

Preparation

  1. Boil rice noodles as directed.
  2. Soak each wrapper in a bowl of hot water about 10 seconds.
  3. Lay ingredients on wrapper leaving 1 inch on right and left sides.
  4. Fold sides. Roll up tightly, placing seam side down on plate.
  5. Cut spring roll in half at an angle.
  6. Wrap strip of seaweed around it when ready to serve.
